Q: Remove the left margin on the blue paragraph, and have it float right instead. What do you think will happen? Will the orange and blue elements sit side by side?

A: I thought the blue paragraph would sit to the right, side-by-side with the orange paragraph. It turned out that is not the case. The blue paragraph goes to the bottom of the orange paragraph instead. I don't know why this is the case.

To have the blue paragraph sit to the right of the orange paragraph, I had to add `width: 80%` and `box-sizing: border-box` to it.
